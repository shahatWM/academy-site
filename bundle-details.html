      Overview

      WaveMaker enables developers to create and reuse custom components across apps, ensuring consistency and accelerating development.

    • Lesson 3

      Prefab Life Cycle

      The life cycle of a WaveMaker Prefab involves creation, configuration, binding to app data, reuse across projects, and updates via version control—ensuring modularity and maintainability in app development.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the prerequisites?

    You just need a Web Browser. If you are looking for exporting into your favourite idea than you will need the entire, developers setup. Like JDK, git, your IDE etc

  • What is WaveMaker Academy?

    WaveMaker Academy is a learning platform designed to help developers master low-code application development using WaveMaker. It offers curated content, hands-on projects, and certifications to build real-world skills in building web and mobile apps.

  • Do I need prior coding experience to start?

    No prior coding experience is required. WaveMaker Academy provides beginner-friendly modules alongside expert-led tutorials, allowing both new and experienced developers to learn at their own pace and grow into full-stack low-code professionals.

  • Are the courses free or paid?

    Many of the learning resources on WaveMaker Academy, including curated bundles and foundational modules, are available without sign-up or payment. Advanced certifications and guided tracks may be part of premium offerings.

  • Will I get a certificate after completing a course?

    Yes. Upon successful completion of select learning tracks and assessments, WaveMaker Academy awards certificates that validate your skills and can be shared on resumes or professional networks.
